Texas Rangers Ugly 2014

Sat 04 October 2014
By JMG

In Sport.

tags: rangers

It's hard to describe the absolute disaster that the Texas Rangers 2014 campain was. As hard as it is to describe, it was even harder to watch, so for the most part I didn't. Maybe that makes me a fair weather fan, but so be it. The final 67 win and 95 loss season was a far cry from the 86 win, 2nd place in the AL West I had predicted at the beginning of the season (here).

The exit of Ron Washington may give the team a chance for a much needed fresh start. I think Tim Bogar would be a decent choice, but I would really like to see Mike Maddux take over as skipper. He has managed to make great strides in getting the best out of pitchers, working in very much of a hitters' park.
